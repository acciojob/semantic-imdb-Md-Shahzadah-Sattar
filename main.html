Inception

Actors Quotes
Inception is a 2010 science fiction action film directed by Christopher Nolan. The movie follows a professional thief who steals information by infiltrating the subconscious of his targets. He is offered a chance to have his criminal history erased as payment for the implantation of another person's idea into a target's subconscious.

Actors

The film stars Leonardo DiCaprio, Joseph Gordon-Levitt, Ellen Page, Tom Hardy, and Ken Watanabe among others.
